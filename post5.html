QuickBooks Payroll Setup Guide

Setting up payroll in QuickBooks can be a game-changer for small businesses. It ensures your employees are paid accurately and on time while staying compliant with tax regulations.

Step 1: Choose the Right Payroll Service

QuickBooks offers various payroll plans—Core, Premium, and Elite. Choose one that fits your business needs, whether you need basic processing or advanced HR support.

Step 2: Enter Company Payroll Information

Provide your business EIN, address, and bank info for direct deposit. QuickBooks will also ask for state and federal tax details.

Step 3: Add Employee Details

Input each employee's full name, address, Social Security Number, pay rate, deductions, and withholding allowances.

Step 4: Set Up Payroll Schedules

Determine how often you pay your employees—weekly, biweekly, or monthly—and align it with your cash flow.

Step 5: Run Your First Payroll

After everything is set, you can preview and run your payroll. QuickBooks will calculate taxes and generate pay stubs automatically.

Bonus: Automate Tax Payments

Enable automatic tax payments and filings to reduce errors and ensure compliance with government deadlines.
