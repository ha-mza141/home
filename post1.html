QuickBooks Online Setup Checklist for Small Businesses

Published: June 5, 2023 7 min read

Setting up QuickBooks Online correctly from the start saves countless hours of cleanup later. As a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor, I've created this comprehensive checklist to ensure you configure everything properly for your business needs.

Initial Account Setup

  • Select the right QuickBooks Online version (Simple Start, Essentials, or Plus)
  • Enter complete company information (legal name, address, tax ID)
  • Set up your fiscal year and tax period
  • Configure user roles and permissions
  • Enable multi-currency if needed

Chart of Accounts

  • Review and customize default accounts
  • Add industry-specific accounts
  • Set up bank accounts, credit cards, and loans
  • Configure payment methods
  • Establish product/service items

Bank Connections

  • Connect all business bank accounts
  • Set up credit card feeds
  • Create bank rules for recurring transactions
  • Establish beginning balances

Sales & Invoicing

  • Customize invoice templates with your logo
  • Set up payment gateways (PayPal, Stripe, etc.)
  • Configure sales tax rates
  • Create recurring invoice templates
