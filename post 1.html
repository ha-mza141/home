10 QuickBooks Online Features You Might Be Missing

Published: May 15, 2023 5 min read

As a QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor with years of experience, I've discovered that many business owners and even some accountants aren't taking full advantage of all the powerful features QuickBooks Online offers. Here are 10 features you might be missing that can save you time and improve your financial management.

1. Recurring Transactions

Stop recreating the same invoices or bills every month. Set up recurring transactions for regular income or expenses, and QuickBooks will automatically create them on your schedule. You can even set rules for how much to increase recurring invoices each year.

2. Bank Rules

Automate your transaction categorization by creating rules for your bank feeds. For example, you can set a rule that any transaction from "Starbucks" should always be categorized as "Meals & Entertainment." This can save hours of manual data entry each month.

3. Custom Reports

QuickBooks comes with standard reports, but you can create completely customized reports tailored to your specific business needs. Save these custom reports for quick access later.

Conclusion

Mastering these features can transform your QuickBooks experience from basic bookkeeping to powerful financial management. If you'd like personalized help setting up any of these features, book a consultation with me.
